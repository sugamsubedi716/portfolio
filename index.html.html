Our Location

New West Point Boarding Secondary School, which is located Beni Municipality of Myagdi district, is a respected private educational institution in Nepal's Gandaki Province. Established in 20499, the school offers comprehensive education from pre-primary levels up to the Ten Plus Two (+2) level, with streams in Management and Education, affiliated with the National Examinations Board (NEB) and approved by the Ministry of Education . The institution is known for its commitment to holistic development, providing facilities such as a well-stocked library, science and computer labs, multimedia resources, and a range of extracurricular activities including sports, dance, music, and educational tours . Additionally, the school offers scholarship schemes to support deserving and underprivileged students, ensuring accessible education for all . With its dedicated faculty and emphasis on both academic excellence and personal growth, New West Point Boarding Secondary School continues to be a cornerstone of quality education in the region
